FAQ

How can I create an account ?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage.

How do I add books to my to be read?

Search for a book using the search bar. Once you find it, click the "Add to TBR", which are books to be read.

Can I leave reviews for books?

Yes! Visit a book's page, scroll down to the review section, and click "Write a Review" to share your thoughts.

Who can access the website ?

Anybody can use this website, this is a safe space for the book community to flourish.

How does the book trending page work ?

All of the books from that category appear there based on users reviews and star ratings.

How do the star ratings work ?

We feel like 1 star is the worst and 5 stars is an amazing read but it is also up to the reader.

Will the website have new features ?

Yes, in the future more features will be added such as reading progress bar and much more.
